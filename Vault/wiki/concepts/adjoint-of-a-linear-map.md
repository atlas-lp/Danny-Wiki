# Adjoint of a Linear Map

A unique linear map T* associated with a linear map T between inner product spaces, defined by the property that the inner product <Tv, w> equals <v, T*w> for all vectors v and w. The adjoint T* of a linear map T can be obtained from its singular value decomposition by interchanging the roles of the orthonormal basis vectors e's and f's.
