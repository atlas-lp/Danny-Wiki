# Analysis of Variance (ANOVA)

A statistical procedure that performs a single overall test for a statistically significant difference among the means of multiple groups. A statistical procedure for analyzing the results of an experiment with multiple groups, serving as an extension of procedures like the A/B test to assess if overall variation is due to chance. A statistical method that involves the decomposition of variance and uses the F-statistic to compare means.
