# Boxplot

A plot based on percentiles that gives a quick way to visualize the distribution of data, also known as a box and whiskers plot. A plot that gives a quick sense of the distribution of data, with the top and bottom of the box at the 75th and 25th percentiles respectively. A plot that illustrates the median, quartiles, and outliers of a numeric variable, often used to compare distributions across different categories.
