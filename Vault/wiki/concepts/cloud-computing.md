# Cloud Computing

A form of service provisioning with common benefits and challenges, based on a series of well-defined concepts, models, technology mechanisms, and architectures. A model for enabling on-demand network access to a shared pool of configurable computing resources that can be rapidly provisioned and released with minimal management effort or service provider interaction.
