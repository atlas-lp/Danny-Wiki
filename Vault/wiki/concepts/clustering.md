# Clustering

A group of independent, interconnected IT resources that work as a single system to reduce failure rates and increase availability and reliability through redundancy and failover. A centrally managed group of connected nodes that process tasks in parallel, enabling distributed data processing, horizontal scaling, and fault tolerance.
