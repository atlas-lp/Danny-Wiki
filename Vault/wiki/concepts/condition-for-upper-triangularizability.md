# Condition for Upper-Triangularizability (5.44)

A theorem stating the necessary and sufficient condition for a linear operator on a finite-dimensional space to have an upper-triangular matrix, which is met if the operator's minimal polynomial factors into polynomials of degree 1 over the base field.
