# Direct Sum

A sum of subspaces where each element has a unique representation as a sum of elements from the constituent subspaces; for two subspaces U and W, this is equivalent to their intersection being U ∩ W = {0}. A sum of subspaces V1 + ... + Vm where the dimension of the sum is equal to the sum of the dimensions of the individual subspaces, analogous to a disjoint union of sets. A sum of subspaces V₁ + ... + Vₘ is a direct sum if and only if the dimension of the sum is equal to the sum of the dimensions of the individual subspaces.
