# Inner Product

A function that takes an ordered pair of vectors to a number and serves as a generalization of the dot product, satisfying properties of positivity, definiteness, additivity, and homogeneity. A function on a vector space that allows for the introduction of geometrical concepts such as length and angle, with specific polarization identities to define it from a norm for real and complex spaces.
