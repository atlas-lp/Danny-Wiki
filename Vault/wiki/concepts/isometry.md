# Isometry

A linear map that preserves norms (||Sv|| = ||v||), which is equivalent to preserving inner products or mapping an orthonormal basis to an orthonormal list. An operator S that can be characterized by all of its singular values being equal to 1, which is equivalent to S*S = I.
