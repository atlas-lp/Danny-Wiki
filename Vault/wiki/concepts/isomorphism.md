# Isomorphism

An invertible linear map between two vector spaces, which signifies they are structurally identical; two finite-dimensional vector spaces are isomorphic if and only if they have the same dimension. A relationship between two vector spaces that are structurally identical, allowing one to be considered a relabeling of the other, such as the isomorphism between R² × R³ and R⁵.
