# Normal Operator

An operator on an inner product space that commutes with its adjoint, meaning TT* = T*T. An operator on an inner product space that commutes with its adjoint ($TT^* = T^*T$). Eigenvectors of a normal operator corresponding to distinct eigenvalues are orthogonal. An operator on an inner product space that commutes with its adjoint. On a complex space, it has a diagonal matrix with respect to some orthonormal basis, as stated by the Complex Spectral Theorem.
