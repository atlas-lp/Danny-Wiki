# Open APIs

Interfaces that allow third parties to access a customer's banking information and initiate transactions on their behalf with consent, expanding digital services beyond a single application. Application programming interfaces (APIs) that inspire innovation, bridge agility gaps, create new business opportunities for financial institutions, and help embed financial services into the day-to-day lives of customers.
