# Orthonormal Basis

An orthonormal list of vectors that is also a basis for a finite-dimensional inner product space, allowing any vector to be written as a linear combination whose coefficients are inner products with the basis vectors. An orthonormal list of vectors that is also a basis for a finite-dimensional inner product space; such a basis is proven to exist for every such space.
