# QR Factorization

A matrix decomposition that expresses a matrix A as the product A = QR, where Q is a unitary matrix and R is an upper-triangular matrix with positive numbers on its diagonal. A factorization of a matrix A into A = QR, where Q is a unitary matrix and R is an upper-triangular matrix with only positive numbers on its diagonal.
