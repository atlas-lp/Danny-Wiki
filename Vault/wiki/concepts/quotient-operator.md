# Quotient Operator

An operator T/U defined on a quotient space V/U by (T/U)(v + U) = Tv + U, which is valid if U is an invariant subspace under T. An operator T/U defined in relation to an operator T and an invariant subspace U. Its minimal polynomial is a divisor of the minimal polynomial of T.
