# Quotient Space

The set of all translates of a subspace U within a vector space V, denoted as V/U. For example, if U is a line through the origin in R², then R²/U is the set of all lines parallel to U. A vector space, denoted V/U, formed from a vector space V and its subspace U, with specific definitions for addition and scalar multiplication on its elements.
