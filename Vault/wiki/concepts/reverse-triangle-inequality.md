# Reverse Triangle Inequality

An inequality for complex numbers w and z which states that ||w| - |z|| is less than or equal to |w - z|. An inequality for operators S and T stating that the absolute difference of their norms is less than or equal to the norm of their difference: | ||S|| - ||T|| | <= ||S - T||.
