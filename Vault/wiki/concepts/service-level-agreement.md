# Service Level Agreement (SLA)

A human-readable document from a cloud provider that describes quality-of-service (QoS) features, guarantees, and limitations of one or more cloud-based IT resources. An agreement that establishes service quality parameters for the use of cloud services and is part of a broader Cloud Services Customer Agreement.
