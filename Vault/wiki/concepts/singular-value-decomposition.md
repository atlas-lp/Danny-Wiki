# Singular Value Decomposition

A key decomposition that is listed as one of the author's ten favorite results in the book. A description of a linear map T from V to W in terms of its singular values and corresponding orthonormal lists in V and W. A method to represent a linear map T from a vector space V to W, where for any vector v in V, Tv is expressed as a sum s_1<v, e_1>f_1 + ... + s_m<v, e_m>f_m, using orthonormal lists e and f and positive singular values s. A representation of an operator T that expresses its action in terms of two orthonormal bases (e_i and f_i) and a set of singular values (s_i).
