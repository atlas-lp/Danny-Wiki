# Skew Operator

An operator B on an inner product space that is the negative of its adjoint, satisfying the condition $B^* = -B$. An operator that equals the negative of its adjoint. A normal operator on a complex inner product space is characterized as skew if and only if all its eigenvalues are purely imaginary.
