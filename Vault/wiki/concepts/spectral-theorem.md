# Spectral Theorem

A crucial result in linear algebra that is listed as one of the author's ten favorite results in the book. A theorem that characterizes operators for which there is an orthonormal basis of the vector space consisting of eigenvectors of the operator. It is divided into the real spectral theorem and the complex spectral theorem.
