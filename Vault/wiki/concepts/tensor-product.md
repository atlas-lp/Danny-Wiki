# Tensor Product

A product operation between two vector spaces V and W that results in a new vector space, V ⊗ W, whose dimension is the product of the dimensions of V and W. The tensor product of multiple vector spaces, V_1 ⊗ ... ⊗ V_m, is defined as the space of multilinear maps on the product of their dual spaces, B(V_1', ..., V_m'), and its dimension is the product of the dimensions of the individual spaces.
