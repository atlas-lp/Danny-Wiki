# Text Mining

The art and science of discovering knowledge, insights, and patterns from an organized collection of textual databases. A technique similar to data mining for which best practices include asking the right question, proposing imaginative hypotheses, and pursuing the problem iteratively. A process for analyzing unstructured text that includes various applications, a specific process, and techniques like the Term Document Matrix.
