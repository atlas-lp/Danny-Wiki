# Unitary Operator

An isometry that maps a vector space to itself, characterized by being invertible with its inverse equal to its adjoint (S⁻¹ = S*). All its eigenvalues have an absolute value of 1. An operator that can be characterized in multiple ways, such as being equivalent to S = 2P - I for an orthogonal projection P, or having all eigenvalues with absolute value 1 under certain conditions.
