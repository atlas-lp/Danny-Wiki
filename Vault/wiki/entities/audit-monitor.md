# Audit Monitor

A mechanism that monitors the type and geographical location of IT resources processing data to ensure fulfillment of legal and regulatory requirements. A mechanism used to track whether data recovery is carried out in compliance with regulations. A monitor that checks whether the relocation of cloud consumer data conflicts with any legal or data privacy regulations or policies.
