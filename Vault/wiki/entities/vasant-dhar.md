# Vasant Dhar

The author of "Thinking With Machines" who describes his personal journey through the different eras of AI, from expert systems to the present. The primary speaker, author, and co-creator of the Damodaran Bot who discusses concepts like agentic AI and trust in machines. A professor who teaches AI and advocates for using machines to learn, emphasizing the importance of curiosity and parental guidance.
