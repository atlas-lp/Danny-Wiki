# Weka

An open-source and freely available stand-alone data mining platform with extensive features, though it may have performance issues with large data. An open-source data mining platform used to produce a visualization of a final decision tree model. A free software platform for data mining applications, also known as Waikato Environment for Knowledge Analysis, developed for academic use.
