## Abstract

This is a recorded NYU Stern Fireside Chat from March 2023 in which Professors Joao Sedoc and Foster Provost conduct a live data science interview with "ChatDS," an AI chatbot avatar powered by a large language model. The interview covers standard data science questions, problem formulation, whiteboard math, live coding, and data visualization to demonstrate what modern AI chatbots can and cannot do. The event serves as both a public stress-test of AI capabilities and an exploration of common criticisms such as factual inaccuracy, fabrication, and lack of true intelligence.
