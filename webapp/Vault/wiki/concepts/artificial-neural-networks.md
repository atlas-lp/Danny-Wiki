# Artificial Neural Networks (ANNs)

Multi-layer non-linear information processing models from AI and machine learning that learn from past data to predict future values. A data mining technique that can achieve high predictive accuracy, often surpassing human experts, but requires a large amount of data for training. A data analytics technique with defined business applications, design principles, and architectures, exemplified by IBM Watson.
