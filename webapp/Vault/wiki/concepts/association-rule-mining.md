# Association Rule Mining

A popular, unsupervised learning technique, also known as market basket analysis, used to identify shopping patterns and find interesting relationships between categorical variables. A data mining technique that works only on nominal data to find rules based on minimum confidence and support levels. A data mining technique used to discover associations in data, with business applications in areas like entertainment and specific methods like the Apriori algorithm.
