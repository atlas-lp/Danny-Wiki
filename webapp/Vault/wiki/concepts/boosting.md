# Boosting

A general ensemble technique that fits a sequence of models where each successive model seeks to minimize the error of the previous one, often by giving more weight to records with large residuals. A class of ensemble models based on fitting a sequence of models, with more weight given to records with large errors in successive rounds. An ensemble method used to create models, which is contrasted with bagging and includes algorithms like Adaboost.
