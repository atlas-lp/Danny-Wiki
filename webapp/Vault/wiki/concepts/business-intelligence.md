# Business Intelligence

A process that includes monitoring the industry, competitors, suppliers, and customers, often using customized dashboards to track key performance indexes (KPIs). An umbrella term for a variety of IT applications used to analyze an organization's data and communicate information to relevant users. A comprehensive set of IT tools designed to support decision making with imaginative solutions for a variety of problems and improve performance in nearly all industries.
