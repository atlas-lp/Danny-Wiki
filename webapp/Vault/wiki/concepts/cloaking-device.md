# Cloaking Device

A proposed mechanism that allows users to inhibit the use of particular pieces of their information in inference, thereby affecting the personal traits that can be predicted about them. A mechanism that allows users to inhibit the use of particular pieces of information in inference, providing control over model-driven decisions without having to stop sharing the information.
