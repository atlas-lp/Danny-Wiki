# Data Wrangling

The process of manipulating, processing, cleaning, transforming, merging, and reshaping datasets. The process of joining, combining, and reshaping datasets to prepare them for analysis. The overall process of manipulating unstructured and/or messy data into a structured or clean form, also referred to as munging. The process of transforming data, which includes combining and merging datasets, handling missing data, reshaping, and string manipulation.
