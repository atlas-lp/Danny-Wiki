# Degrees of Freedom

A statistical concept explaining the use of n-1 in the denominator of the variance formula when making estimates about a population from a sample. A parameter that allows the t-distribution to adjust to different sample sizes, statistics, and numbers of groups. The number of values free to vary in a statistic calculated from sample data, which is used as a parameter in many statistical tests and probability distributions.
