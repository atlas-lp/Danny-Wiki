# Dimensionality Reduction

The process of finding a small subset of dimensions in a high-dimensional dataset that captures most of the variation, which can help clean data and enable the use of other techniques. A technique to remove features by distilling vectors of several hundred numbers down to a handful of important dimensions. A technique recommended for use before applying nearest neighbors in higher dimensions to mitigate the curse of dimensionality. A technique in data science, for which principal component analysis is one method, that addresses the 'curse of dimensionality'.
