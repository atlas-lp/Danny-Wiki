# Evidence Counterfactual

An explanation method that reveals the particular evidence, such as Facebook Likes, that caused a predictive model to make a specific inference about an individual. An explanation technique for a model's decision that shows how the outcome would have changed if specific pieces of evidence, such as a user's 'likes', had been absent from the data record.
