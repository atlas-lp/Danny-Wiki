# F-Distribution

A distribution used to test multiple treatments across groups for measured continuous values, based on the F-statistic which is the ratio of variability among group means to variability within each group. A standard reference distribution to which an F-statistic is compared to determine whether differences among treatment means are greater than expected by random chance.
