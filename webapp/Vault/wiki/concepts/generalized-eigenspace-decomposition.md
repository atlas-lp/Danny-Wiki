# Generalized Eigenspace Decomposition

A major result stating that a finite-dimensional complex vector space V can be decomposed into the direct sum of the generalized eigenspaces of an operator T on V. A decomposition of a vector space that is used to prove the existence of square roots for invertible operators and to extend the Jordan basis result from nilpotent to general operators.
