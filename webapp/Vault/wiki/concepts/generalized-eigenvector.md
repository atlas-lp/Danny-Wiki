# Generalized Eigenvector

A vector used to form a basis for a finite-dimensional complex vector space for a given operator, especially when the operator does not have enough eigenvectors to form a basis. A non-zero vector `v` corresponding to an eigenvalue `λ` of an operator `T`, which is introduced to handle cases where an operator does not have enough eigenvectors to form a basis.
