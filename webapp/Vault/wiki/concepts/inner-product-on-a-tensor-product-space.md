# Inner Product on a Tensor Product Space

A unique inner product on the tensor product V ⊗ W of two inner product spaces, defined such that the inner product of two pure tensors ⟨v ⊗ w, u ⊗ x⟩ is ⟨v, u⟩⟨w, x⟩.
