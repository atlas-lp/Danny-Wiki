# Orthogonal Complement

For a subset U of a vector space V, the orthogonal complement, denoted U-perp, is the set of all vectors in V that are orthogonal to every vector in U. The set of all vectors in an inner product space V that are orthogonal to every vector in a given subset U; this set, denoted U-perp, is always a subspace of V.
