# Predictive Modeling

A data science concept used to develop models for predicting outcomes, such as loan defaults, as part of a larger analysis. A process for creating models to forecast outcomes, which is used in the case study to inform investment decisions and improve portfolio performance. A technique used to make inferences about individuals' personal traits from their data, such as Facebook "Likes", in order to optimally target consumers and voters.
