# Resampling

The process of taking repeated samples from observed data, which includes both bootstrap and permutation procedures. A general term for procedures that draw repeated samples from observed data, which includes both bootstrapping (with replacement) and permutation procedures. The process of drawing additional samples, referred to as resamples, from an observed data set.
