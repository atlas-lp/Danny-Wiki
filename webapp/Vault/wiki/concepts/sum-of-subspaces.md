# Sum of Subspaces

The set of all possible sums of elements from a collection of subspaces, which is itself the smallest subspace containing all the individual subspaces. For subspaces V1 and V2 of a vector space V, their sum V1 + V2 is the smallest subspace of V that contains both V1 and V2.
