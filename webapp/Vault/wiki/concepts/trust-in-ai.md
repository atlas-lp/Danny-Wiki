# Trust in AI

A major open question concerning when and why AI systems should be trusted, especially in domains like finance where a small but consistent edge can be valuable despite imperfect accuracy. The willingness to rely on an AI system, which is based on its predictability and the low cost of its errors rather than its absolute correctness.
