# ATN (Advanced Telecom Networks)

A global company that supplies network equipment to the telecommunications industry and is used as a case study. A telecommunications company that is developing a cloud computing adoption roadmap and has experience migrating applications to a PaaS platform. A company that decided not to proceed with its cloud migration plan based on the results of a risk assessment concerning ETelReg compliance. A case study company that developed applications using a leased PaaS environment and tested the auto-scaling functionality of its cloud services. An organization from a case study that expands its ready-made environment architecture by using the state management database mechanism.
