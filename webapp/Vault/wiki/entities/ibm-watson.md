# IBM Watson

A system that absorbs medical research to make probabilistic diagnoses in the form of a decision tree, along with explanations for its recommendations. A system deployed at healthcare institutions to provide potential treatment options and help clinicians uncover insights from patient information in electronic medical records (EMR). A system that uses UIMA to process massive amounts of data, which famously beat a Jeopardy champion and is now used for business applications like healthcare diagnostics. A system from IBM presented as a caselet example for the application of Artificial Neural Networks in medicine.
