# Innovartus

A case study company that uses a cloud provider's audit monitoring system to collect data on the geographical origin of users to comply with software licensing restrictions. A company that supports biometric scanners and MFA systems to ensure secure access for parents to cloud-based accounts related to their children's products. A company that is leasing two cloud-based environments from different cloud providers to establish a pilot cloud-balancing architecture for its cloud service. A company that moved from a regional cloud provider to a large-scale global provider to support its growth, addressing issues like data recovery and application migration.
