## Abstract

This is an audiobook preview of *Data Science for Business: What You Need to Know about Data Mining and Data-Analytic Thinking* by Foster Provost and Tom Fawcett, narrated by Benjamin Lange. The preview covers the book's preface, an introduction to data-analytic thinking, and the chapter on business problems and data science solutions. The book is a foundational text connecting data science concepts to business applications, emphasizing how organizations should think analytically about data. The preview was published via Google Play Books in February 2024.

## Key Claims

- Data science and data mining require a specific mode of thinking called "data-analytic thinking"
- Business problems can be framed and addressed through data science solutions
- Understanding data mining concepts is essential for business practitioners, not just technical specialists
- The book bridges the gap between data science techniques and their practical business applications
- The preview covers introductory and foundational material from the first two chapters plus the preface
